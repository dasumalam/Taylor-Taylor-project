About

This is Best Team in our Londan-Salon

Our award winning hair salon in Portobello Road is positioned in the heart of Notting Hill..

Our West London hair salon is positioned on the corner of Oxford Gardens at number 309 Portobello Road, right in the heart of Notting Hill. Sitting firmly between the independent shops on the Golborne Rd and the luxurious boutiques on Westbourne Grove our third London Hairdressing Salon is the perfect complement to our Shoreditch Styling team..

Take your seat at our signature cocktail bar or relax in our tea lounge before you are whisked into a subterranean washroom where our hand selected team will translate their knowledge and experience into a hairstyle or hair colour which is tailored directly for you.
